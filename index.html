In my vision for Bangladesh 2.0, I call for a transformative new system that moves beyond survival to eliminate poverty entirely. My propose is replacing traditional capitalism with Social Business—enterprises designed to solve problems, not maximize profits. My urge is the unleashing of youth as job creators, leveraging technology as a true equalizer, and placing women at the center of this new architecture. This collective national project aims to build a just, innovative, and inclusive society that serves as a global model.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
